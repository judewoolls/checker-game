Instructions

Checkers is a two-player game where each player has 12 pieces. The goal is to capture all of the opponent's pieces or block them so they can't move. The game is played on an 8x8 board with alternating dark and light squares. The pieces are placed on the dark squares and can only move diagonally. If a piece reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a king and can move in any direction. The game ends when one player captures all of the opponent's pieces or blocks them from moving.
